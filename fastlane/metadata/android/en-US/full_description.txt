MixedMeter is a focused Android metronome for musicians who want something clean and practical — no ads, no clutter.

<b>Main metronome</b>
• Set BPM with a circular dial
• Choose time signature and note value
• Tap beat boxes to cycle lead, beat, and inactive accents
• Optional subdivisions (2–5 per beat) with a separate tone
• Change accents or tones during playback without interrupting timing
• Add the current meter to a sequence from the bottom bar

<b>Sequence mode</b>
• Build multi-step practice routines with repeats per step
• Tempo percent scaling and optional loop mode
• Scroll map for long sequences; tap to jump to a step
• Save and load named sequences

<b>Settings</b>
• Separate lead, beat, and subdivision tones (built-in clicks plus WAV samples)
• Multiple visual themes

<b>Information</b>
• In-app how-to, plus email, Discord, and GitHub links

Navigate with two-finger swipes between the main screen, Sequence, Settings, and Information.
